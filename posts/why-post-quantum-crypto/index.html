Thu, Apr 1, 2021

Hi everyone! In this post I talk a little about why is important to start thinking about post-quantum cryptographic algorithms in order to keep our security up-to-date with the development of quantum computers. Hope you like!

Post-quantum cryptography: Is is something to eat?

We have an expression in brazilian portuguese - “It is something to eat?” - used in situations where someone talks about something non-trivial. It means that the other person or other people don’t even have a superficial knowledge of the conversation topic.

That appears to be the case of post-quantum cryptography. When I explain to my friends - which are not familiar with the computing - they seem to have a similar reaction. At the very least, they associate with quantum physics, which makes them think it’s a knowledge even distance from them.

The main reason of this post is to clarify a little about what the heck is post-quantum cryptography and how this subject can, somehow in the near future, relate to our personal lives.
